Buttermilk

The gentle joy of a timeless tradition.

INGREDIENTS
Water
Curd
Salt

What’s Special About This Drink?

Buttermilk is the quiet hero of the Indian table. It’s what we reach for when we need to feel balanced, cooled, and settled. We’ve kept it exactly as it should be: fresh, house-made curd churned with water and a pinch of salt. No additives, no flavorings—just the honest, creamy simplicity that has been a staple in our homes for centuries.

What makes Shake & Gulp Buttermilk special is its simplicity and honesty—no artificial additives, just a time-tested recipe bottled for modern life. And because real ingredients naturally settle, every bottle is meant to be shaken fresh before drinking.

Health Benefits

  • Actively helps cool the body and restore balance in heat
  • Naturally rich in probiotics to support a happy, healthy gut
  • Provides gentle, light hydration without any heaviness
  • Acts as a natural digestive aid after spicy or complex meals
  • A low-calorie, nutrient-dense way to refresh yourself daily

Gentle daily wellness, just like it’s been for generations.

Who Should Drink This?

Buttermilk is for everyone—a daily drink for balance and refreshment.

  • Daily after lunch to help your system process the meal
  • When you feel that internal 'heat' or acidity after a long day
  • If you're looking for a simple, savory refresh that feels like home
  • On sweltering afternoons when your body needs honest hydration
  • Anyone seeking a mild, comforting drink that’s kind to the stomach

A simple drink that your stomach will thank you for.

Emotional Connect

It’s the glass served after lunch at home, the cooling comfort on a summer afternoon, the quiet remedy your grandmother trusted more than anything else. Shake & Gulp brings that same feeling back — familiar, soothing, and made with care.

Shake & Gulp captures that gentle joy and puts it in your hands — familiar, comforting, and made with care.

How to Enjoy
